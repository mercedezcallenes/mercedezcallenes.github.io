LADS Students and Graduates

Students

Students in the Latin American Development Studies (LADS) minor engage in interdisciplinary research and experiential learning that prepares them for careers in policy, development, and academia.

Many students participate in faculty-mentored research projects, conference presentations, and community engagement initiatives. Their work reflects a commitment to social justice, sustainability, and regional expertise in Latin America.

Student Spotlight: Students have presented their research at national conferences such as NCUR, showcasing projects on agriculture, gender, migration, and development.
